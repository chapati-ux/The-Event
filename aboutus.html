How It All
Started

Our story began with a determined college student who faced endless struggles managing registrations for his campus events. From incomplete data to last-minute chaos, the process was overwhelming. Frustrated by the lack of a streamlined solution, he decided to turn his problem into an opportunity. With a vision to simplify event registrations and management for schools, colleges, and local events, The Happening Events was born.

The Happening Eventsis not just about registrations we're experts in full-scale event management too. From planning and logistics to execution and post- event follow-ups, our experienced team ensures every detail is handled with precision. Whether it's a fashion show, workshop, sports event, or seminar, we deliver events that leave a lasting impact
